VISION

SSS Police Department is committed to delivering the most efficient, effective, productive, and quality police service to the community. We are dedicated to advancing a cooperative partnership with our community to develop better community policing, improved communications, and reduced crime. We shall strive to develop a comprehensive strategy to resolve public safety issues and enhance the quality of life within our community. The SSS Police Department is an open, friendly, and community-minded organisation devoted to quality public service, unyielding in purpose and dedicated to living by values reflecting a genuine desire to care for the safety and well-being of the public.
